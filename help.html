Sending Feedback Messages

Sending a message on my websites is very simple.

First, locate the contact form or message section. This is found in the home page:

  • Contact section

Just type your message in the text area and click the send button.

After sending, your default email application will open with your pre-filled message to me, and you can add any additional details before sending the email.

Uploading your website to GitHub

To upload files to GitHub, open your repository and click "Add file".

Then choose upload files, drag your files in, and commit changes.

GitHub Pages will automatically update your website after a few minutes.

Customizing Your Website

You can customize your website using HTML and CSS.

Change colours, fonts, layout, and images inside your style.css file.

Always refresh your browser after changes to see updates.
